About Me

My name is Matthew and I am looking forward to a career as a web developer where I can apply my skills finding solutions that create meaningful and memorable user experiences. Since my first exposure to the digital world many years ago, I have always been fascinated with the rapid pace of technology advances, and how technology changes our lives….for better or for worse. I am keen to understand how technology works “under the hood”. Closing the gap between knowing and not knowing what I don’t know richly satisfies me.

I have a significant portfolio of knowledge around coaching and training. I am big believer in developing people to make operations run more efficiently. Problem solving with a strategic and tactical approach is a skill I used. Asking questions and active listening have been an invaluable asset to getting the job done and forging strong relationships.
